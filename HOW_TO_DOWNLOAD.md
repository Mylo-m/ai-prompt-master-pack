## 📥 How to Download

### Option A: Download Single File
1. Click on `500_AI_PROMPTS_COPY_PASTE.txt` above
2. Click the **"Raw"** button
3. Right-click → **"Save As..."** → Save to your computer

### Option B: Download Everything (ZIP)
1. Click the green **"Code"** button above
2. Click **"Download ZIP"**
3. Extract the ZIP and open the files

## 🚀 Quick Start

1. **Open** `500_AI_PROMPTS_COPY_PASTE.txt`
2. **Copy** any prompt (Ctrl+C / Cmd+C)
3. **Paste** into ChatGPT, Midjourney, or Suno
4. **Replace** `[BRACKETS]` with your info
5. **Press Enter** and enjoy!
